Digital Decadence

Inspired by Net Art Anthology "skinonskinonskin"

Visit SKINONSKINONSKIN

Separated by the land and the seas

To my distant lover…how shall I appease?

For lack of physical intimacy

I crave your digital familiarity

Regular phone-ins

Become our version of skin on skin

Entanglement in your binary digits and wires

Merely satisfies my burning desire

Day and night I browse your files

Forgetting between us the thousands of miles

Traversing the metaverse

Suddenly your words become terse

Our connection becomes unstable

“I love you” replaced by “Are you able?”

Communication infrequent

Where are thou the Romeo to my Juliet?

Alas our digital love is decadent

For digital despair is evident

Status mortality and you’re forever offline

Digital media permanence reminds me of when you were once mine
